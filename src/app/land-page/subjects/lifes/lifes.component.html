Importance of science in ICT
ICT can enhance teaching and learning in the primary science lesson and while ICT capability is embedded in the Science learning area, you as a teacher can make the most of this opportunity and develop ICT capability within the context of science.


How is science related to ICT?
The use of ICT in science education offers even more advantages due to the attractive premises to simulate and interactively explore and test experiments which would be too expensive or too dangerous in real settings (for example in nuclear physics).


How is science used in computers?
They allow us to analyze huge data sets and run statistical analyses far quicker than in the past. Scientists also use them to run simulations and build electronic models


How does science and technology help students?
